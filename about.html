Melody Serwah

UI / UX Designer

User-centered design is my priority.


Hi, my name is Melody. I am a UX/UI Designer in training based in Canada. My educational career expands on design thinking, prototyping, user-research, web design, graphic design, information architecture and other ux-related areas. I am passionate about problem solving and designing products and systems that are usable, inclusive and attractive.

As a young and emerging designer, my design philosophy is attractive, human-centered design. I believe that good design stems from the value provided to the user, and the value is determined by how much the user's needs are prioritized throughout a design from start to finish. I also believe that design should be attractive, aesthetically pleasing and evoking.

In my free time I enjoy listening to music, watching tv shows and spending time on social media, especially Twitter.
